How to Cook Wagyu

Cooking wagyu for the first time can be scary as it's such an expensive and high-quality cut of meat. Most people aren't exactly used to preparing it. We've prepared some recipes for you to try with our products to make yourself a nice meal. Here are the recipes.

Recipe 1: Basic Steak

Here we have a basic steak recipe. Applying this recipe to a wagyu cut of steak will allow you to make a very basic but incredibly tasty steak. The wagyu steak, in the end, is still just a steak, so cooking it like any other steak to your preference results in an incredibly well-made dish.

Recipe 2: Wagyu Sandwich

This recipe is for what's called a wagyu katsu sandwich. It takes a wagyu steak and cooks it to perfection, making it into a small sandwich with a katsu sauce. While this is a simple dish to make, the quality of the meat makes the flavor of the sandwich so rich and the meat melts right in your mouth from its tenderness. With our wagyu, the meat is tender enough for the steak to be cooked whole as shown in the video.

Additional Recipe 1: Wagyu Roast Beef Bowl

Wagyu Roast Beef Bowl: This recipe creates a beef bowl with rice, beef, and other ingredients. The recipe is quick and easy and works really well with wagyu beef.

Additional Recipe 2: Wagyu Steak

Wagyu Steak: This is an actual wagyu steak recipe that aims to take a high-quality wagyu cut and make it into a mouth-watering steak for you to enjoy in only half an hour.
