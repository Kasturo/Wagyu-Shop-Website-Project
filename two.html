About Our Halal Wagyu Brand

What is Wagyu

Wagyu by definition means Japanese cow, it is used to refer to four breeds of cow that are native to Japan. Authentic wagyu meat is deemed to be so special due to its rich flavor, its fat marbling and its distribution of fat. Wagyu cows are held to a high standard, this is to ensure its quality, this makes wagyu some of the highest quality meat in the world.
